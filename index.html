HOW TO PLAY
🎯 GOAL: Hit targets, earn points, survive!
🔫 GUN MODE — The Bar
   Shoot moving bottles (water, wine, soda) as they slide by.
   💣 Watch for BOMBS — shoot them for a chain explosion bonus!
   Miss a bottle = lose 1 ❤️. You have 3 hearts.
🏹 ARCHER MODE — Medieval Castle
   Aim arrows at the apple on the runner's head.
   ✅ Hit apple = +20 pts & +2 bonus arrows!
   ❌ Hit the runner body = lose 1 ❤️ (3 hearts total).
   Run out of arrows = game over! Arrows shown in HUD.
🎯 DARTS MODE — The Dojo
   Throw weapons at falling fruits from above.
   Weapons cycle: Dart → Shuriken → Spear → Sword (→ Chair on Normal).
   Smaller/rarer fruits = more points! ★ = rare fruit!
   Miss a fruit = lose 1 ❤️. You have 3 hearts.
⚙ DIFFICULTY
   🟢 Normal — Relaxed speed, Chair weapon available
   🟠 Hard — Faster targets, fewer arrows in Archer
   🔴 Devil — Max speed, fewest arrows, double scoring
