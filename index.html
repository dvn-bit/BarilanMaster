HOW TO PLAY
🎯 GOAL: Hit targets, earn points, survive!
🔫 GUN MODE — The Bar
   Shoot moving bottles as they slide by.
   💣 BOMBS = chain explosion bonus!
   Miss a bottle = lose 1 ❤️. You have 3 hearts.
🏹 ARCHER MODE — Medieval Castle
   Aim arrows at the apple on the runner's head.
   ✅ Hit apple = +20 pts & +2 bonus arrows!
   ❌ Hit body = lose 1 ❤️. Run out of arrows = OVER!
🎯 DARTS MODE — The Dojo
   Throw weapons at falling fruits from above.
   Weapons cycle: Dart → Shuriken → Spear → Sword.
   ★ = rare fruit! More points! Miss = lose ❤️.
📱 MOBILE: Tap anywhere on screen to fire!
   Use the 🎯 fire button or tap the game area.
